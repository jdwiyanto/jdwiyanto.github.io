Hi! Jacky here.

I am a data professional specializing in machine learning and data analytics and have worked in the education and healthcare technology sectors since 2018. As a computational biologist with a PhD in microbial genomics, I have a strong foundation in statistical analysis and modeling as well as programming. This ensures that all models and analytics I build are statistically sound and fundamentally solid, capable of delivering accurate forecasting and data-driven decision making. I possess a broad range of skillsets: familiar with Python, SQL, R, Markdown, Tableau, GCP, AWS and GitHub, to name a few.

Some of my previous work in the healthcare technology sector includes developing and validating random forest models capable of predicting health and demographic status from genomic and metagenomic datasets, and developing an optimal probiotic formulation to support certain health conditions based on available literature.

This portfolio contains the models and analyses I have conducted to solve real-world business problems. I also occasionally post analytics and programming tutorials and walkthroughs as a way of giving back to the community, from which I have learned and will continue to learn.
